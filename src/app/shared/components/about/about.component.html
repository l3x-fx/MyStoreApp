About MyStore

Allow me to introduce my latest Full Stack project. MyStore is a showcase of my skills in FullStack development. built using a modern technology stack. It reflects my commitment to creating responsive web solutions that combine form and function seamlessly. Below are some of its standout characteristics:

  • On the front end, I utilized TypeScript and Angular to create a dynamic and responsive user interface.
  • For state management, I employed the power of NgRx to ensure an efficient data flow throughout the application.
  • On the back end, I harnessed Node.js and Express to develop a robust and performant server.
  • The application's data is stored and managed in a PostgreSQL database, ensuring data integrity and reliability.

MyStore encapsulates my dedication to creating visually captivating and highly functional websites. It's not just a project; it's a testament to my commitment to delivering exceptional user experiences.
